About The Festival

Jauna Muzika is a yearly festival presenting artists working with sound and music as an experimental art form.

It is one of the oldest and most changed music festivals in Lithuania, founded in 1992. As the concept of music changed, maintaining its relevance so did the festival itself, its directions, and sometimes its names.
